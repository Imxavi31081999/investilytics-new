Team

Shefali Sihag
CEO, Founder of InvestLytics

Shefali has over 13 years of professional working experience having worked with industry heavyweights such as Deloitte, Acies Consulting, Marcus Today Investment Strategists and the Commonwealth Bank of Australia.
  • Bachelors of Business Administration (specializing in Risk Management) – Monash University Australia
  • Masters in Applied Finance – Monash University, Australia
  • Masters in Banking and Finance law – University of Melbourne, Australia
  • and CFA - Level 1.
Shefali started her career in back-office operations for a leading bank in Australia where she internalized bank policies, customer interactions, lending and compliance requirements for home & corporate loans, etc. Later she would move on to work with a leading Australian wealth management firm, eventually building and administering a management process that would meet the firm’s compliance requirements.

After coming to India in 2016, while working as a Deputy Manager with one of The Big Four in financial risk consulting, Shefali became intimate with the complexities of investment management and the gap in the Indian market regarding an independent and comprehensive wealth management platform.

The realization ultimately led her to establish InvesLytics in Feb 2020.

Vishal Raheja
Advisor, InvestLytics

Vishal has in-depth knowledge in the field of Indirect Taxes having worked with Taxmann - India's #1 source for everything on Tax & Corporate Laws for over 6 years.

Over the years, Vishal has authored various articles in major publications on controversial issues under GST, Service Tax, VAT/CST, etc, and has been the guest speaker for ICAI, Tax Bar Association and Industrial Federations on multiple occasions.
